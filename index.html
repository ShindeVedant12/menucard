Chicken Tandoori
₹350

Chicken Tandoori is a marinated and roasted chicken dish that originates from the Indian subcontinent. The chicken is typically marinated in a mixture of yogurt and a blend of aromatic spices, including cumin, coriander, turmeric, garam masala, ginger, and garlic. This marinade not only imparts a vibrant red color to the chicken but also infuses it with a complex and savory flavor profile.

Butter Chicken
₹550

Butter Chicken, also known as Murgh Makhani, is a delectable and iconic dish that has its roots in North Indian cuisine. Renowned for its creamy texture, rich flavor profile, and tender pieces of chicken, Butter Chicken has become a beloved staple in Indian restaurants around the world. The dish features boneless chicken pieces that are marinated in a mixture of yogurt and a blend of spices, including garam masala, cumin, coriander, and turmeric. After marination, the chicken is traditionally cooked in a tandoor or oven, giving it a slightly charred and smoky flavor. However, it can also be prepared on the stovetop.

Chicken Biryani
₹529

Chicken Biryani is a quintessential South Asian dish featuring marinated chicken layered with aromatic basmati rice and fragrant spices. The chicken is tenderized in yogurt and spices, while the rice is infused with saffron and whole spices. The layers are slow-cooked or dum-cooked to perfection, resulting in a flavorful one-pot delight. Garnished with fried onions, fresh herbs, and often served with raita, Chicken Biryani is a celebrated culinary masterpiece enjoyed for its rich flavors and cultural significance.

Pizza
₹350

Pizza is an iconic and universally loved dish originating from Italy. It consists of a round, flattened dough base topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings, often including vegetables, meats, and herbs. The pizza is baked in an oven, resulting in a delightful combination of crispy crust, gooey melted cheese, and flavorful toppings. Its versatility has led to countless variations globally, making pizza a beloved comfort food enjoyed in various styles around the world.
